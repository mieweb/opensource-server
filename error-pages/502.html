502

Service Unavailable

A service is configured for this hostname, but the upstream is not responding.

Troubleshooting

  • Check that the service is running inside the container.
  • Verify the configured port matches the port your service is listening on.
  • Ensure the service binds to 0.0.0.0 (all interfaces), not 127.0.0.1 (localhost only).
  • Check that no firewall rules inside the container are blocking incoming connections.
