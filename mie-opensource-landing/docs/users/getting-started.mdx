# Getting Started

To use the cluster, you need an account on the web interface at <ContainerCreationUrl><ContainerCreationUrl /></ContainerCreationUrl>.

## Registering with an Invite

If an admin sent you an email invite, click the link in the email. Your email will be pre-filled and locked. Fill in your name, username, and password, then submit. Your account is active immediatelyâ€”you can log in right away.

## Registering without an Invite

Click **Register** on the login page and fill in all fields (first name, last name, username, email, password). Your account will be created with **pending** status. An admin must approve it before you can log in. You'll receive an email once approved.
